Chen Wang (王琛)

I am an incoming CIS PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. Lingjie Liu. My research interest lies in computer graphics and computer vision, especially 3D generation.

I graduated with a M.S. in CS from Tsinghua University in June, 2023, working with Prof. Shi-Min Hu and Prof. Song-Hai Zhang. Before that, I received a B.E. in CS and a B.A. in Economics from Tsinghua University in June, 2020.
