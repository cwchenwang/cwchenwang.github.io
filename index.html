Chen Wang  王琛

I am a CIS PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania starting from Fall 2023, where I have the pleasure to work with Prof. Lingjie Liu. I am affiliated with the Graphics Lab and GRASP lab. I am also a member of AnySyn3D research group. My research interests are computer graphics and computer vision, with a focus on 3D/4D generative models and neural 3D representaions.

I did my undergraduate and master study in CS at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Shi-Min Hu and Prof. Song-Hai Zhang.
