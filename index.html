Chen Wang (王琛)

I am now a CS master student at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shi-Min Hu and Prof. Song-Hai Zhang. My research interest lies in computer vision, computer graphics. My research goal aims to help machines to perceive the 3D world, which involves 3D scene understanding, modelling and interaction.

I received my Bachelors' degree in CS and Economics (Second Major) from Tsinghua University in June, 2020.
